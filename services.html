How the dog walking works

Simple, reliable and built around your dog: door-to-door pickup, a long harbour walk with the pack, and a happy, tired dog home again.

Your dog's day, step by step

Four simple steps

1

Doorstep pickup

Steven collects your dog from home between 9:30 and 12:00 — no need to drop off anywhere.

2

Down to the harbour

Your dog joins a friendly, well-matched pack for the trip to the seafront.

3

The big walk

Around three hours of sniffing, playing, socialising and fresh sea air.

4

Home & happy

Dropped safely back to your door, settled and ready for a well-earned nap.
