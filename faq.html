Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know about walks with Alpha Dog Ireland — times, breeds, areas, booking and payment.

What time do you pick up?

The walk starts somewhere between 9:30 and 12:00, depending on the day's route. Steven collects your dog straight from your door — no drop-off needed.

What time do you drop off?

Your dog is out for around three hours in total, so drop-off depends on the pickup time. A 9:30 pickup is usually home around 12:30, and a 12:00 pickup around 3pm — so drop-offs fall roughly between 12:30 and 3pm.

Do I need to be home?

No — that's the beauty of it. Lots of clients simply leave a key or a lockbox. Steven lets himself in, collects your dog, heads off for the walk and drops them back afterwards, however you'd like.

Where do you walk?

The pack walks at Apna Park in Dún Laoghaire — around the back of the West Pier, facing Seapoint and right beside Salthill Beach. It's a lovely bit of open space by the sea, perfect for a proper group walk. You'll find it on the map below.

What breeds do you walk?

All breeds are welcome, whatever the age or size, as long as your dog is happy in a group. Please just let us know anything specific we should know about your dog beforehand — health, temperament or anything they're funny about — so we can look after them properly.

What towns do the regular dogs come from?

The regular pack already comes from Blackrock, Dún Laoghaire, Monkstown, Foxrock, Deansgrange, Cabinteely and Sallynoggin, plus the wider South Dublin area. If you're nearby but not on the list, just ask.

Do you walk at weekends?

The structured pack walks run Monday to Friday, around the middle of the day. For weekend walks, just ask and Steven will let you know what he can do.

Do I have to book in advance?

Not necessarily. If you call or text in good time, Steven can usually organise a next-day pickup. That said, most clients simply book in for the whole week.

How would you like to pay?

Cash, card or bank transfer — whatever suits you. Payment is usually at the end of each week on Friday. If there are only three walks in a particular week, payment is on the last walk of that week.

How do I book?

Easiest is to give Steven a call or a text / WhatsApp on 087 360 9741. He'll check availability in your area and get your dog booked into the pack.
