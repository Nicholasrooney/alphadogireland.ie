Meet Steven & Ayra

A dog walker who actually loves the job

Steven Jeffers has been walking South Dublin's dogs for over ten years. Every dog — young pup, senior, big or small — gets patience, care and a genuinely good walk.

His own dog Ayra comes along on every walk and keeps the whole pack calm and in order. It's how nervous or excitable dogs settle in so quickly.

  • Calm, experienced handling of every temperament
  • The same trusted face at your door every day
  • Ayra, the four-legged deputy, keeping the peace
